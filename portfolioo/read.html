Design Thinking

Design thinking is a process in which a designer undergoes through five
different steps → Empathize, Define, Ideate, Prototype and Test to understand
the users problems better and come up with better solutions using different methods.

EMPATHIZE — Understanding The Users

Empathizing is all about understanding the users better by asking the users
different questions about their experiences, challenges, etc. Also, observing their behaviours
to draw much more insights about their feelings in using a product. .

This is done by different methods like interviewing the users one on one, taking online surveys, collecting the data which is already there in the form of articles etc. The method that a UX designer choose depends on the problem they are trying to solve or a product they are designing. For my project, I have prepared the following questions to interview my users……..

1. can you tell me about yourself?
2. why you get a thought that try something new other than traditional products?
3. Duration of replacing your doors and windows or varnishing wooden doors?
4. What is the expected rate of the product you are choosing in your new home?
5. Is your window long lasting to the climatic condition?
6. Is your doors facing distortion?
7. Are you facing problems such as noise problem from outside?
8. What is the first memory comes to your mind while thinking about doors and windows? can you explain it?
9. Is your doors and windows striking? you have any suggestion for its modification?
10.What type of windows that you expect to your home in future? What are the consideration you put on that?
